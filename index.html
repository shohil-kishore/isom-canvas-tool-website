Privacy Policy

We do not knowingly collect any information about you or from Canvas.

To provide downloadable student lists, the ISOM Canvas Tools extension uses your existing authenticated session on the University of Auckland's Canvas website to retrieve and process information from the Canvas API. None of this information is stored by us. None of this information is intentionally sent anywhere except to you in the file download. Depending on which Canvas site you are on, all Canvas API calls are made securely over HTTPS to:

- canvas.auckland.ac.nz, or
- auckland.instructure.com, or
- auckland.beta.instructure.com, or
- auckland.test.instructure.com.

This policy was last updated on October 11, 2019.
